About

I am a guitarist, first and foremost.

I have played in many different ensembles comprised of many different instrumentations as well as being a solo performer. I am classically-trained and play a variety of styles, including Flamenco, Bossa Nova, Classical, Pop and Rock.

As a performer, I try to convey the essence of the music to the listener, whether it's a light-hearted Bossa Nova groove or a haunting, Impressionist melody. There is nothing more pleasing to me than a positive reaction from my listeners.

Lessons

I am a passionate music teacher with experience in helping students deepen their appreciation and understanding of music while developing their playing abilities.

I have been a private guitar instructor since 2002, and was trained at the Brooklyn College Conservatory. I studied classical technique, and it provided me with the tools to be able to analyze any technical problem, be it right hand, left hand, fingerstyle, or pick. I have experience with both young children and adults. I teach my students to be active listeners, to be able to identify the building blocks of the music they like and to be able to manipulate them into their own musical vocabulary.

I am a creative and flexible teacher. I always make sure the lessons are fun and engaging. I spend time finding out what my studentsâ€™ goals are and help them develop a challenging but realistic plan for achieving those goals. For younger students who may not have concrete goals, I show them how music can enrich their lives and help them develop at their own pace. My primary goal As a teacher, is to help students develop the tools for teaching themselves.
